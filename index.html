About Me

Hello! My name is Ganyuan. You can also call me "Gan".

I am a doctoral research at Télécom Paris.

My research is about cryptography and my research interests include:

  • Provable Security: Game-Based Proofs, Composable Security, Security Abstraction/Formalism...
  • Cryptographic Primitives: Homomorphic Encryption, zkSNARKs, MPC, and their compositions...

I also snowboard and practice Aikido (both beginner level).

Feel free to reach out :)
